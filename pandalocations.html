Panda Locations

Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding

This sanctuary in China is home to dozens of pandas and focuses on breeding, conservation, and education. Visitors can observe pandas in spacious, naturalistic enclosures.

San Diego Zoo

The San Diego Zoo has been a pioneer in panda conservation and research. The pandas have recently returned as well with revamped enclosures!

Edinburgh Zoo

As one of the only zoos in the UK with giant pandas, Edinburgh Zoo offers a specialized enclosure and is actively involved in conservation efforts.

Vienna Zoo is renowned for its panda conservation program. Its exhibit combines natural landscaping and excellent viewing areas. Pandas returning early next year!

Ocean Park Hong Kong

This park offers a beautiful panda habitat with modern facilities, making it a popular attraction for tourists and locals alike.

Beijing Zoo

The Beijing Zoo is famous for its historic panda exhibit. The enclosure mimics the natural bamboo forests of their native habitat, making it a must-visit location for panda lovers.

Smithsonian's National Zoo

Located in Washington, D.C., the Smithsonian's National Zoo features a world-renowned panda enclosure. The lush habitat includes bamboo forests and natural terrain, perfect for observing these majestic animals.

Chongqing Zoo

Known for its expansive panda enclosures, Chongqing Zoo provides a unique opportunity to see pandas in a well-maintained and tranquil setting.
