Welcome to Panda Explorer

Discover fascinating facts about pandas and find locations where you can see them!

Pandas, with their iconic black-and-white coats, are the rockstars of the animal kingdom. They’re devoted to a bamboo diet, spending most of their day feasting on it. Despite their laid-back appearance, pandas are surprisingly tough—skilled climbers and even capable swimmers, with a unique “thumb” (a special wrist bone) that helps them grip bamboo. Classified as "vulnerable," pandas face threats from habitat loss and poaching, making their conservation essential. These charismatic, bamboo-loving creatures need our help to ensure their survival.
